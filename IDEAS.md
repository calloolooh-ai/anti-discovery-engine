# Moonshot Hackathon — Project Ideas & Elevator Pitches

## Judging Weights (what matters)
- Originality & Zero-to-One Thinking: **35%**
- Technical & Scientific Depth: **25%**
- Long-Term Vision & Impact: **20%**
- Feasibility & Execution Path: **10%**
- Prototype & Demonstration: **10%**

Idea + paper > demo polish. One day timeline — go in with a clear concept.

---

## 1. The Anti-Discovery Engine
**Domain:** Science / AI

Every scientific field has blind spots — questions nobody asks because the framing itself is wrong. This system maps the topology of human knowledge (papers, citations, concepts) to find the edges where nobody is looking. Not "what's the next paper" but "what question, if asked, would make 10 fields collide." A compass for civilization-scale discovery.

**Build:** arxiv knowledge graph + citation network + LLM to identify conceptual gaps and generate high-leverage unexplored questions.

---

## 2. Biological Ledger
**Domain:** Biology / Data

DNA stores 215 petabytes per gram and lasts 10,000+ years without power. Encode critical human knowledge into synthetic DNA strands embedded in living organisms (bacteria, seeds) that survive civilizational collapse. A distributed, self-healing, biological archive of everything humanity knows.

**Build:** Simulate the encoding/decoding pipeline, encode a text corpus into DNA sequences, design the error-correction scheme.

---

## 3. The Forgetting Machine
**Domain:** AI / Neuroscience

Every AI suffers catastrophic forgetting — learn new things, old things vanish. The human brain doesn't, because of strategic forgetting during sleep consolidation. Build a neural network architecture that deliberately forgets — discarding low-signal memories, consolidating high-signal ones via a sleep cycle analog — solving one of the foundational unsolved problems in ML from first principles.

**Build:** Custom training loop with a "sleep phase" that prunes/consolidates weights, benchmarked against standard continual learning tasks.

## 4. Moral Compiler
**Domain:** Governance / AI / Philosophy

Every society operates on stated values. Every society has internal contradictions between those values that only surface during crisis. This system takes a formal statement of values, automatically detects logical contradictions, and generates the minimum value updates to achieve coherence. A compiler for ethics.

**Build:** Formalize moral axioms as logic statements, use SAT solver + LLM to find contradictions and propose resolutions, demo on a real constitution or charter.

---

## 5. Civilizational Immune System
**Domain:** Economics / Network Science

The 2008 crash, COVID supply chain collapse, internet outages — all spread the same way: contagion through hidden network dependencies. This system models global systems (financial, supply chain, energy) as networks and detects fragility patterns — identifying which single nodes, if they fail, cascade into civilizational collapse — before it happens.

**Build:** Network graph of real supply chain / financial linkage data, contagion simulation, visualization of systemic risk hotspots.

---

## 6. Language Death Reversal
**Domain:** Linguistics / Cognitive Science

A language dies every two weeks. We're not just losing words — we're losing unique cognitive frameworks: ways of perceiving time, color, causality that exist in no other language. This project reconstructs the cognitive architecture of dying languages and asks: what problems could humanity solve if we thought in Pirahã? In Ayapaneco?

**Build:** Model trained on linguistic relativity research + endangered language corpora, extracts unique grammatical/conceptual structures, generates "cognitive lenses" for users.

---

## 7. Sleep Architecture Compiler
**Domain:** Neuroscience / HCI

Targeted Memory Reactivation (TMR) — playing sounds associated with learned material during slow-wave sleep — demonstrably improves retention. Systematize TMR into a programmable interface: declare what to remember, the system encodes it into audio cues, monitors sleep stages via wearable, delivers cues at peak consolidation windows. Programming human memory.

**Build:** Sleep stage detection via mic/accelerometer, cue scheduling algorithm, demo with measurable retention improvement on a learning task.

---

## 8. Adversarial Legislature
**Domain:** Governance / AI

Every law has unintended consequences. None are caught before passage because nobody is tasked with being maximally adversarial to proposed legislation. This system plays permanent devil's advocate — simulating second and third-order effects using agent-based modeling, game theory, and historical precedent — generating the strongest possible case against every policy before it passes.

**Build:** LLM + agent-based simulation + historical policy outcome dataset, demo by stress-testing a real historical law (e.g., Prohibition) and showing what the system would have predicted.

---

## 9. Synthetic Intuition
**Domain:** AI / Philosophy of Science

Current AI is trained on answers. Scientists generate breakthroughs through the process of being confused and resolving confusion. Train a model not on knowledge, but on the trajectory from confusion to insight: scientific notebooks, failed experiments, the messy pre-discovery process. An AI that generates hypotheses rather than retrieves answers.

**Build:** Curate pre-discovery scientific reasoning dataset, fine-tune model to generate hypothesis chains, benchmark on unsolved problems.

---

## 10. First Contact Protocol
**Domain:** Mathematics / Communication / AI

When we encounter genuinely alien intelligence — advanced AI or extraterrestrial — we have no shared language, substrate, or evolutionary history. What's left: mathematics and physics are universal. Build a communication protocol grounded purely in mathematical structure: a self-bootstrapping language that any sufficiently intelligent system could decode from first principles alone.

**Build:** Design the protocol formally, implement encoder/decoder, demonstrate that a zero-prior-knowledge system can reconstruct meaning from the signal alone.
