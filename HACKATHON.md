## Theme & Philosophy

> "Build what does not exist yet. Pursue ideas worthy of decades, not days."

Zero-to-one innovation — ideas that sound unrealistic, technologies that should not exist yet, systems capable of changing how humanity lives. **Not** optimizing existing solutions. Creating entirely new categories, capabilities, and futures.

Rewards **depth over polish**, **vision over trend-following**, **originality over imitation**.

---

## Submission Requirements

Three components required:

1. **Prototype / Demonstration**  
   Code, simulations, hardware, models, or experimental systems demonstrating the concept.

2. **Moonshot Paper**  
   Technical blueprint covering:
   - The problem
   - Gaps in existing solutions
   - First-principles insights
   - Scientific foundations
   - Implications
   - Envisioned future

3. **Vision Presentation**  
   Addressing: clarity of thought, reasoning depth, technical ambition, innovative conviction.

## Judging Criteria

| Criterion | Weight |
|-----------|--------|
| Originality & Zero-to-One Thinking | 35% |
| Technical & Scientific Depth | 25% |
| Long-Term Vision & Impact | 20% |
| Feasibility & Execution Path | 10% |
| Prototype & Demonstration | 10% |

**Originality details:** First-principles reasoning, contrarian insights, new category creation.  
**Technical depth:** Engineering complexity, scientific rigor, computational sophistication.  
**Vision/Impact:** Potential influence on technology, science, society, infrastructure.
